Mosswood

Mosswood is a cozy survival game set in a beautiful, untouched world filled with lush forests, serene lakes, and towering mountains. Explore diverse biomes, discover hidden structures like firewatch towers and forest cabins, and gather resources to survive. Build your home and thrive in this peaceful yet challenging environment.
